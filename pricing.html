FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Starter packages typically take 3–5 business days. Standard packages 7–14 days. Premium packages 3–6 weeks depending on complexity. We always deliver on the agreed timeline.

Do I need to provide content for my website?

You'll need to provide your business information, logo, and images. We can help with copywriting and suggest stock images if needed — just let us know when you get in touch.

Can I update the website after it's built?

Absolutely! We build websites with content management in mind. Standard and Premium packages include CMS so you can update content yourself. We also offer maintenance packages.

Do you offer hosting and domain registration?

We can help you set up hosting and domain registration through our recommended providers, or we can deploy to your existing hosting. We'll guide you through the entire process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, eSewa, Khalti, and bank transfer. We typically require 50% upfront and 50% on project completion.
